Who We Are

A UK Holding Company Built on Purpose

Cedar Enterprises is a privately held UK company with interests spanning commercial construction and the wholesale trading and distribution of soft drinks. We are registered in England and Wales and operate with a clear mandate: quality, reliability and integrity in everything we do.

Our holding structure means we take a considered, long-term view of the businesses we operate — investing in people, processes and partnerships that create lasting value.
