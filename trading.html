Our Businesses

Trading & Distribution

Trading Division

Connecting Brands to the British Market

Cedar Enterprises' trading division operates as a wholesale sourcing and distribution business for soft drinks and beverages. We work with brands, importers and regional producers to ensure their products reach the retail and hospitality sectors efficiently and reliably.

Our trading model is built around relationships — with our suppliers, our distribution partners, and the end clients who depend on consistent, competitively priced stock. We take the complexity out of the supply chain and deliver results.
