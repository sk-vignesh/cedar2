Who We Are

About Cedar Enterprises

Our Identity

A Holding Company Built on Long-Term Thinking

Cedar Enterprises is a privately owned UK holding company, incorporated and registered with Companies House in England and Wales. We exist to develop and manage businesses that create genuine, lasting value.

Our two operating divisions — construction and soft drinks trading — reflect a deliberate strategy of diversification. We bring the same values to both: professionalism, accountability and a commitment to the people and partners we work with.

We are a young, ambitious company. Our registered office is in Epsom, Surrey, and our ambitions reach far beyond it.
